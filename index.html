Giorgia Maurovich is a writer translator and researcher active in the cultural, artistic and literary sphere between Italy and Slovenia. She has a background in Foreign Languages, majoring in German and Polish, is currently in the field of Comparative Literature and at the moment she’s working on the first collection of literary essays. When not thinking about literature, her interests are cities, exile, marginality, multilingualism, decay and memory.
If you need more information contact her.
